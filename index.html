Frequently Asked Questions

We know how important it is to have the right information. Here are the answers to some of the most common questions.

How early should I book my rentals?

For the best selection, we recommend booking at least two weeks in advance. However, we may accommodate last-minute bookings depending on availability.

Do you deliver outside Lagos?

Yes, we cater to events across Nigeria. Extra delivery charges may apply depending on the distance.

What happens if I need to cancel my booking?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. Please contact us at least 48 hours before the event for full details.

Do you offer event setup and takedown?

Yes! We handle everything from delivery to setup and takedown, so you can focus on enjoying the event.
